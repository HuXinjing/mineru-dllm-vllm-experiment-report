PDF 3: InfinFlow Company History

InfinFlow is the company behind the open source projects Infinity and RAGFlow. Public profiles describe InfinFlow as an AI infrastructure provider headquartered in Shanghai, China, founded in 2023.

The team's early work focused on the data layer needed for retrieval augmented generation. In late 2023, InfinFlow open sourced Infinity, an AI native database designed to support RAG style workloads, including fast search and efficient indexing. Soon after, the company expanded from database infrastructure into an end-to-end RAG application stack.

On April 1, 2024, InfinFlow open sourced RAGFlow as a complete RAG engine. The project emphasizes deep document understanding, a robust ingestion pipeline, and retrieval that can support enterprise use cases. In practice, this history reflects a move from building the foundation, storage and retrieval, to delivering a full developer platform. Today, InformiFlow positions RAGFlow as a context layer for AI agents, combining ingestion, indexing, retrieval, and chat or agent workflows into one platform. The company continues to publish tutorials, release notes, and improvements that track the fast pace of the open source ecosystem.
